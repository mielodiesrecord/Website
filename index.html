About

Not just a label this is family.
Powered by friendship, fueled by sound.
Changing the Music Industry for Artist and Producers

Independent record label founded by Miel.

  • Read Bio

    • Mielodies Records is a forward-thinking independent label committed to discovering and supporting innovative artists who challenge the norm. Established in 2025, we have been promoting cutting-edge sounds spanning Pop, Hip-Hop, Indie, and Dance, delivering music that resonates with audiences worldwide. Based in Davao City, we take pride in fostering a vibrant environment where creativity flourishes and diverse voices are celebrated. Driven by a dedication to quality, authenticity, and progressive production, Mielodies Records proudly supports everything from emerging local talent to rising international acts.

    Featuring early releases from artists like Davenzo and Reminiscne, the label is steadily gaining recognition for advancing artistic expression and empowering the creators behind the music. At Mielodies Records, every song tells a unique story, and every artist's voice deserves to be heard, as we continue shaping the future of music, one release at a time.
